What this includes

The Care Packet can print one day, a 7-day weekly packet, or a custom date range. It pulls from appointments, transportation windows, active medications, open tasks, important directory contacts, and optional notes.

It is designed to be simple enough to print and hand to a driver, caregiver, or family member.
